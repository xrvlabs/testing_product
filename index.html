    DentWerk 3D Model 4 Resin is a high-performance photopolymer resin specifically developed for dental model fabrication using advanced 3D printing technologies. Designed to deliver exceptional accuracy and detail, it ensures precise reproduction of dental structures, making it ideal for study models, diagnostic setups, and treatment planning.

    Compatible with LCD, DLP, and SLA 3D printers operating within a wavelength range of 365-405 nm, this resin offers versatility across multiple digital workflows.
