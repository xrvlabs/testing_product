    Frequently Asked Questions
    How can I track my order?
    You can easily track your order by logging into your profile and visiting the 'Orders' section. A tracking link is also sent via email once the order is dispatched from our warehouse.
    What is the return and replacement policy?
    We offer a 10-day replacement policy for defective or damaged products. Please ensure the product is unused and in its original packaging while initiating the return.
    Do you offer bulk discounts for clinics?
    Yes, we have a dedicated B2B program for dental clinics and hospitals. Please reach out to our support team at info@gridlabs.com with your requirements for special pricing.
    How do I redeem my reward coins?
    Reward coins are automatically applied as a discount on your cart value during checkout. Make sure you are logged into your account to avail the benefits!
