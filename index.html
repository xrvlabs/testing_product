    RETURN & REFUND POLICY

    LAST UPDATED: 24 APRIL 2026

    1. GENERAL RETURN POLICY

    At Oraco Dental Innovation, we strive to ensure you receive the highest quality dental products. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we offer a hassle-free 10-day return policy for eligible items from the date of delivery.

    2. CONDITIONS FOR RETURN

    To be eligible for a return or replacement, the following conditions must be met:

    • The product must be unused, in its original condition, and strictly in its original packaging.
    • All tags, seals, warranty cards, and accessories included with the product must remain intact.
    • A valid proof of purchase (invoice or order ID) must be provided.
    • In case of a defective or damaged product upon arrival, you must report it to our support team within 48 hours of delivery with photographic evidence.

    3. NON-RETURNABLE ITEMS

    For hygiene and safety reasons, certain categories of dental products cannot be returned or exchanged once dispatched or opened:

    • Sterile products, composites, cements, and opened resins.
    • Handpieces, endomotors, or equipment with broken warranty seals.
    • Customized or special-order dental equipment.
    • Products sold under final clearance or specific promotional sales.

    4. REFUND PROCESS

    Once your returned item is received and inspected at our warehouse, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

    • Approved Refunds: If approved, the refund will be processed and automatically credited back to your original method of payment (Credit Card, UPI, Net Banking) within 5-7 business days.
    • OracoCoins: If you used OracoCoins for the purchase, the equivalent coins will be refunded to your wallet account.
    • Shipping Costs: Original shipping costs are non-refundable. If you receive a refund, the cost of return shipping may be deducted from your refund unless the return is due to our error (e.g., wrong or defective item).

    5. ORDER CANCELLATION

    You may cancel your order free of charge before it has been dispatched from our warehouse. Once the order status changes to 'SHIPPED', standard return policies will apply. To cancel an order, please visit the 'My Orders' section in your profile or contact our support team immediately.

    6. NEED HELP WITH A RETURN?

    If you have any questions about returning an item or need to initiate a replacement request, please reach out to our support team at dentaloraco@gmail.com or raise a ticket from the 'Help' section in your Profile Dashboard.
